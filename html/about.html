Betrayal at House on the Hill Has 3 Different Editions

Betrayal at House on the Hill is a popular board game that was first released in 2004. It has since been expanded with additional editions, each introducing new content and gameplay mechanics.

The game consists of two distinct phases:

  • Exploration Phase: Players work together to explore the house, revealing new rooms and items, while trying to uncover clues about the upcoming betrayal.
  • Betrayal Phase: One player turns against the group, and the game shifts into a battle for survival, where the other players must try to survive and stop the betrayerâ€™s plan.

Betrayal at House on the Hill has released three editions over the years:

  1. Original Edition (2004)

    The original edition introduces players to the eerie House on the Hill. Players explore the house, but eventually, one player betrays the group, and the rest must survive while discovering the traitor's plans.

  2. Betrayal at House on the Hill: Widow's Walk (Second Edition, 2016)

    This edition added the "Widow's Walk" expansion, which introduced new rooms, haunts, and scenarios, increasing the replayability of the game.

  3. Betrayal Legacy (2018)

    Betrayal Legacy is a campaign-style game that builds a continuous story over multiple sessions. Each game affects the next, creating a long-term evolving narrative about the House on the Hill.

  4. Betrayal at House on the Hill: 3rd Edition (2022)

    Betrayal at House on the Hill: 3rd Edition is the latest version of the game, featuring different characters, new cards, and other exciting changes that enhance the gameplay experience.
