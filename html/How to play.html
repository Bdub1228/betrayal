Betrayal at House on the Hill: Game Overview

Game Overview

A 3-6 player board game where players explore a haunted house, uncover its rooms, and encounter spooky events. The game unfolds in two phases:

  • Exploration Phase: Players cooperate to explore the house.
  • Haunt Phase: A "Haunt" is triggered, turning one player into a traitor, and the game becomes a battle between the traitor and the remaining heroes.

Each playthrough is unique due to 50 different Haunts and a modular game board.

Objective

Before the Haunt: Explore the house, collect items, and grow stronger.
After the Haunt:

  • The traitor works to achieve their sinister goal.
  • The heroes try to stop the traitor and complete their own objective.

The winner is determined by which side accomplishes their goal first.

Game Setup

  1. Place the starting tiles (Hallway, Entrance Hall, and Grand Staircase) in the center.
  2. Also set out the Basement Landing and Upstairs Landing out.
  3. Shuffle the room tiles and create a face-down stack.
  4. Set aside the Traitor's Tome and Secrets of Survival books for the Haunt phase.
  5. Place the monster cards, tokens, and dice nearby.
  6. Players choose a character:
    • Each has a double-sided board with stats: Might, Speed, Sanity, and Knowledge.
    • Give players matching bases and clips for their stats.
  7. Players start in the Entrance Hall.

How to Play

Phase 1: The Exploration Phase

  1. Move: Move up to the number of spaces equal to your current Speed.
  2. Explore New Rooms:
    • If you move through a doorway without a room, draw and place a room tile that matches the current floor.
    • Align the tile's doorways with existing rooms and follow its instructions:
      • Event: Draw and resolve an Event card.
      • Item: Draw an Item card.
      • Omen: Draw an Omen card, then roll for a Haunt.
  3. Roll for the Haunt:
    • When you draw an Omen card, roll the same number of dice as Omen cards that have been drawn.
    • If the total is more than 5, the Haunt begins.

Phase 2: The Haunt

When the Haunt begins:

  • Determine the traitor using the Traitor's Tome and scenario details.
  • Split the players:
    • The traitor reads their instructions.
    • The heroes read the Secrets of Survival.
  • The traitor and heroes take turns trying to achieve their objectives.

Winning the Game

The heroes or the traitor win by completing their specific Haunt objectives, which vary with each scenario.
