Product Features:

Exciting Third Edition

This edition of the Betrayal at the House on the Hill game is rich with horror-based content, gaming elements, and characters not seen in previous versions.

Miniatures Board Game

The game includes 6 finely detailed character figures, each with a removable colored disc, and features 12 diverse characters not seen in previous editions.

Modular Board Pieces

The Betrayal at House on the Hill tabletop game features 50 blood-curdling scenarios. Each trip to the house promises fresh horrors and a new story is created every time you play.
